Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

The short version: HabitGrid stores everything on your device. No account, no tracking, no servers — except one: when you unlock Pro, your license key is verified against our payment provider. That's it.

What we collect

Almost nothing. HabitGrid is a local-first app — all your data lives in your browser's localStorage. This includes:

  • Your habit names and private notes
  • Your daily check-in history
  • Your name (if you set one in Profile)
  • Your chosen accent colour
  • Your Pro status and license key

None of this is sent to any server. It never leaves your device.

License verification

When you activate a Pro license key, the app sends that key to our server (hosted on Vercel), which checks its validity with our payment provider Dodo Payments. The key is not stored on our server. This is the only time any data leaves your device.

Analytics

We use Vercel Analytics to count page visits. This collects anonymised, aggregated data (page views, country, device type). No cookies. No personal identifiers. No cross-site tracking.

Payments

Payments are processed by Dodo Payments. When you purchase Pro, your payment details (card number, email) are handled entirely by Dodo — we never see or store them. Dodo's privacy policy applies to the checkout process.

Data deletion

To delete all your data: open your browser settings and clear site data for HabitGrid. Everything is gone instantly. There is nothing on our servers to delete.

Children

HabitGrid is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes

If this policy changes materially, we'll update the date at the top of this page.

Contact

Questions? Reach out at hello@habitgrid.app or open an issue on GitHub.
